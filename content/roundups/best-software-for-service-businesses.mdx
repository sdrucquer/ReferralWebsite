## The Recommended Stack

For most service businesses, this stack covers daily operations:

1. Gusto for payroll
2. Jobber for scheduling and field workflows
3. QuickBooks for accounting
4. QUO for business phone

## Why This Stack Works

Each product handles a distinct operational layer. Together they reduce manual work and improve execution speed.

## Implementation Order

1. Payroll first
2. Accounting second
3. Field workflow platform
4. Communication tooling
