## Overview

OnPay is a payroll platform known for simplicity and strong value for cost-conscious small businesses.

## Key Features

- Payroll and tax filing
- Basic HR support
- Straightforward setup

## Pricing

OnPay is usually priced competitively and appeals to teams that want predictable costs.

## Pros

- Good value
- Easy onboarding
- Clean core payroll workflow

## Cons

- Lighter feature ecosystem
- Fewer advanced capabilities than broader platforms
- Less depth for larger organizations

## Who It Is Best For

Small businesses focused on affordability and simplicity.

## Our Verdict

A strong budget option, especially for teams that do not need advanced add-ons.
