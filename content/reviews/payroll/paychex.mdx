## Overview

Paychex Flex is a full-service payroll option with HR-related add-ons and support for growing businesses.

## Key Features

- Payroll and tax services
- Time and attendance modules
- Optional HR support

## Pricing

Pricing varies by package and optional add-ons.

## Pros

- Reliable payroll infrastructure
- Useful bundle options
- Suitable for established teams

## Cons

- Can be expensive versus SMB alternatives
- Interface feels less modern than newer competitors
- Feature clarity depends on package structure

## Who It Is Best For

Companies that already use Paychex services or want bundled payroll and HR tools.

## Our Verdict

A solid provider, but many early-stage businesses may prefer lower complexity and clearer pricing.
