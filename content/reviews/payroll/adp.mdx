## Overview

ADP Run is a long-standing payroll platform often selected by businesses that want a legacy provider with broad service options.

## Key Features

- Core payroll processing
- Tax filing support
- HR and compliance add-ons

## Pricing

Pricing is generally quote-based and can vary depending on team size and required features.

## Pros

- Strong brand history
- Broad payroll capability
- Works for complex setups

## Cons

- Can feel heavier for small teams
- Less transparent pricing than SMB-first tools
- Onboarding can be slower

## Who It Is Best For

Businesses that want an established provider and are comfortable with a more enterprise-style experience.

## Our Verdict

ADP Run is capable, but many small teams will find faster time-to-value with simpler payroll-first tools.
