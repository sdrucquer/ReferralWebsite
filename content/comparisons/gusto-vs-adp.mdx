## Quick Verdict

For most small businesses, Gusto is the better choice because it is easier to set up, easier to operate weekly, and more transparent on pricing.

## Comparison Summary

Use ADP if you need enterprise-like service depth from day one. Choose Gusto if you want fast, practical payroll operations with less friction.

## Final Recommendation

Start with Gusto unless your compliance requirements are unusually complex.
