## Quick Verdict

OnPay is a strong value play, while Gusto is the better all-around platform for long-term growth.

## Comparison Summary

If budget is your top concern, OnPay is worth serious consideration. If you want broader capabilities with a polished experience, pick Gusto.

## Final Recommendation

For most teams planning to scale, Gusto remains the safer long-term choice.
