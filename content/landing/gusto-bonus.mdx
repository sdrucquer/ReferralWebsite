## Why I Recommend Gusto

I run payroll for a real team every pay cycle, so I care about two things: reliability and simplicity.

Gusto delivers both. It gives small business owners a clean workflow for onboarding, payroll processing, tax filing, and employee access to pay documents.

## Bonus Qualification Checklist

To qualify for the $200 gift card:

1. Sign up through the referral link.
2. Set up your business and payment details.
3. Add at least one employee (or yourself).
4. Run first payroll.
5. Watch for gift card delivery around 30 days later.

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

- Signing up outside the referral link
- Not finishing payroll setup
- Delaying first payroll for too long

If anything looks wrong with referral tracking, contact Gusto support and mention Iconic Cleanup LLC (`iconiccleanup@gmail.com`).
